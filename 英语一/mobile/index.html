📖 阅读理解（40分）

2024 Text 110分 🎯 出题人考察点

细节题、主旨题、推断题、词义题、例证题（4篇×5题×2分）。

真题（节选）：

The concept of "intellectual property" has a long history, dating back to the Venetian Republic's 1474 patent statute. Yet it was not until the 19th century that the modern patent system took shape in the United States and Europe. In the contemporary digital age, intellectual property faces unprecedented challenges from artificial intelligence, open-source movements, and global information flows.

问题：What can be inferred from the first paragraph?

  • A. Intellectual property is a recent invention.
  • B. The US patent system predates European systems.
  • C. Modern IP faces new challenges in the digital era. ✅ 答案
  • D. AI has solved IP disputes globally.
🎯 解析：① A 错：long history ≠ recent invention。② B 错："took shape in US and Europe" 无先后。③ C 对：unprecedented challenges 直接对应 new challenges in digital era。④ D 错：faces challenges ≠ solved disputes。
得分点：定位关键词 0.5分 + 识别同义改写 1分 + 排除绝对化 0.5分（满分2分）
⚠️ 踩坑：被 A "recent invention" 字面意思误导；混淆 took shape 时间。

📝 完形填空（10分）

2023 完形20题×0.5分 🎯 出题人考察点

实词辨析（动词/名词/形容词）+ 虚词（介词/连词）+ 固定搭配。

Researchers have long debated whether AI will (1) ____ human jobs. Some predict a(n) (2) ____ future where automation eliminates routine work, while others argue new technologies historically (3) ____ more jobs than they destroy.

(1) A. replace B. enhance C. complement D. challenge
(2) A. bleak B. bright C. steady D. unprecedented
(3) A. destroy B. transform C. create D. require
答案：A A C
(1) replace：AI 替代人类工作
(2) bleak：与"乐观"形成对比（while 暗示）
(3) create：固定搭配 create more jobs than they destroy
💯 4 大套路：① 首句原则 ② 上下文线索（so/but/because）③ 固定搭配 ④ 复现原则。

🌐 英译汉（10分）

2024 翻译5句×2分 🎯 出题人考察点

长难句拆分、词汇精准选择、中文语序调整。

原文：The ability to synthesize information from diverse sources and to make connections across seemingly unrelated fields has become increasingly valuable in modern workplaces.
标准译文：在现代职场中，从不同来源整合信息以及在不同领域之间建立联系的能力变得越来越有价值。
踩分点：① synthesize → 整合（不是"合成"）② make connections → 建立联系 ③ increasingly → 越来越 ④ 整体通顺（4分）
💯 5 大得分技巧：① 拆主干 ② 译修饰 ③ 调语序 ④ 选词准确 ⑤ 写人话。
⚠️ 丢分重灾区：synthesize 译成"合成"（应"整合"）；seemingly 漏译"看似"。

✍️ 小作文 · 应用文（10分）

2024 小作文10分 🎯 出题人考察点

格式（2分）+ 内容（3分）+ 语言（3分）+ 连贯（2分）。

真题：作为学生代表，写邮件向教授申请论文延期 1 周，说明理由并表达感谢。

Dear Professor Smith,

I am writing to request a one-week extension on my thesis submission deadline.

Due to unexpected circumstances including a two-week illness, I have fallen behind on my schedule. An additional week would enable me to submit a more thorough thesis.

I sincerely apologize for any inconvenience. I will provide a medical certificate and updated progress report.

Thank you very much for your time and understanding.

Yours respectfully,
Li Ming
高分要点：格式正确（Dear + Yours）2分；内容完整（理由+道歉+感谢）3分；语言准确（request / unexpected circumstances）3分；语篇连贯（Due to / however）2分。

📝 大作文 · 议论文（20分）

2024 大作文20分 🎯 出题人考察点

图画描述（5分）+ 寓意阐释（7分）+ 个人观点（8分）。

真题：图画显示两条路：一条平坦拥挤，一条荆棘但标着"创新"。评论寓意。

【第一段·描述图】The picture depicts two divergent paths. One is wide and crowded—a symbol of the conventional route. The other is narrow, overgrown, and labeled "Innovation", with only a solitary figure daring to venture along it.

【第二段·阐释】This captures a defining tension: comfort of conformity vs. risk of pioneering. Throughout history, breakthroughs—from printing press to AI—have emerged from those willing to challenge conventional wisdom.

【第三段·观点】As university students, we should recognize that true growth lies beyond comfort zones. We ought to cultivate critical thinking and courage to explore uncharted territories.
💯 5 段式黄金模板：① 描述图景 3 句 ② 揭示寓意引主题 ③ 举例 + 理论分析 ④ 个人观点 ⑤ 展望未来。
必备高分词：define / capture / emerge from / despite / contribute to。
⚠️ 常见丢分：① 大段套模板被识破（反模板扣分）② 时态错乱 ③ 通篇简单句 ④ 跑题。
